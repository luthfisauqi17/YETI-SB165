Features

Suspension

Yeti has increased the progressivity of the SB165 to 27% (SB150 is 15%.) to tune it to coil shock applications.

Handlebar

Yeti believe in big forks not balance, the SB165 runs a huge 180 mm fork on the front.

Saddle

However, the biggest news is the new 77-degree seat tube, up a whopping 4 degrees from the SB6c. This gives the new Yeti SB165 a far more efficient climbing position.
